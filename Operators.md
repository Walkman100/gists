### Divide (true)
Normal division: `50 / 12 = 4.1̇̇6`

### Divide (floor)
No remainder: `50 \ 12 = 4`

### Modulus division
Only the remainder: `50 Mod 12 = 2` (remainder is still part of the fraction, so `2/12 = 0.1̇̇6`)
